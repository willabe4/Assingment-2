Vault Hunters!

Hyperion are hard at work making Pandora and the rest of this great universe a safer, more peaceful place to exsist in. How do you, the great rogue warriors of the vaults, fit into this? It just so happens Handsome Jack, our handsome leader, is after a vault himself and needs the best vault hunters there is to offer! Being the generous company we are, Hyperion is willing to aid and assist in the search for a vault, and is evening willing to share the wonders and treasures the vault may offer.

Any strong, abled vault hunter who is keen on this offer must go to Pandora and take the next express train to the Arctic Wasteland where Handsome Jack himself wll be there to greet and breif you.

We are definitely not planning to kill any of you!
